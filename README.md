# IoT-Automated-Solar-Tracking-System
IoT based automated solar tracking system using Arduino and LDR sensors to optimize solar panel efficiency.

## Overview
The IoT-based Automated Solar Tracking System is designed to automatically adjust the position of a solar panel according to sunlight intensity. The system uses sensors and a microcontroller to maximize solar energy absorption by continuously tracking the direction of maximum sunlight.

## Features

- Automatic solar panel direction adjustment
- LDR-based sunlight detection
- Servo motor control
- Improved solar energy efficiency
- Real-time sunlight tracking
- IoT-based monitoring capability

## Hardware Components

- Arduino Uno 
- LDR Sensors
- Servo Motor
- Solar Panel
- Battery
- Jumper Wires
- Breadboard

## Software Requirements

- Arduino IDE

## Working Principle

The LDR sensors detect sunlight intensity from different directions. The microcontroller compares the sensor values and controls the servo motor to rotate the solar panel toward the direction with maximum sunlight.

This helps increase the efficiency of solar energy generation compared to fixed solar panels.

## Applications

- Solar energy optimization
- Smart renewable energy systems
- IoT-based energy monitoring
- Sustainable power generation
